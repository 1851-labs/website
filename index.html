The opportunity

The internet made billions of people consumers. AI is about to flip that. The cost of creation has collapsed — but no one has built the platform that makes it the default behavior.

GenTube is a remix-first, social AI art platform. It's free. It generates in ~2 seconds. Users don't just create — they build on each other's work. We think that's how a billion people get into creation: by making it feel more like play than production.

We're moving fast and the problem is still wide open. The people who join now will shape what this becomes.
