Machine Learning Engineer

The models are the product: what people can make is what they'll play with. You'd own them end to end across a GPU fleet serving millions of images a month, from how fast they feel to how good and surprising the results are.

New models come out constantly; you'd evaluate them fast, ship the ones that make creating more fun, and find the ones that unlock things people couldn't make before.

Designer

Creation should feel like play, not a form.

You'd design the core create flows and ship them in code with the engineers. Experiments decide: cut what doesn't move the numbers, double down on what does.

You should ship in code, live in the data, and feel what users feel: where they get stuck, what makes them come back. Iterate in hours, not weeks.

Tools: Figma, React/CSS, PostHog.
